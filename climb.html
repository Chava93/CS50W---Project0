Climbing in Mexico City

Parque los dinamos


Mexico City is a great place to be a climber. There are lots of climbing gyms with very accessible prices. Also, there are are many possibilities to climb outdoor in any kind of style: sport climbing, bouldering and even traditional climbing.

My favorite climbing spot is named "Los Dinamos". This is a national park 30 minutes away from the city where you can climb the whole year with the advantage of the closeness of the spot and the delicious food you can take after your training!
