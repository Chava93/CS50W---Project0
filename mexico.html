Mexico City

Mexico City is my favorite place, some of the reasons are the diversity of festivals that it hosts. Next I'm going to name some of my favorite ones:

  • Festival of Tamal
    The "Tamal" is a typical Mexican dish. At this festival, you can try different "tamales" from all around the country and even the world! This is because this dish also exists in other countries from South America with many different styles.
  • International friendly cultures faire
    This festival looks like a big market place where every country in the world has a spot. They show what their typical mores are and you can try some of their typical dishes.
  • Eurojazz
    At this festival, famous jazzists from Europe come to Mexico and give free concerts. Here you can hear world-class jazzists while taking a picnic.
